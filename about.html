About Me

Welcome to my corner! I'm Nathan, a long-standing game developer with a passion for crafting immersive experiences in the vast realms of science fiction. With years of hands-on experience as a design lead, concept artist, and level designer, I've journeyed through virtual galaxy after galaxy, bringing dreams to life one pixel at a time.


Design Lead

As a design lead, I thrive on the challenge of turning abstract ideas into tangible game mechanics and narratives. I orchestrate the creative process, guiding teams to conceptualize, iterate, and refine until we've crafted an experience that resonates deeply with players. From brainstorming sessions to late-night debugging, I'm there every step of the way, ensuring that our vision shines through in every aspect of the game.


Concept Artist

When I'm not wearing my design lead hat, you'll often find me lost in the depths of my sketchbook, conjuring worlds and characters that defy imagination. As a concept artist, I specialize in breathing life into the fantastic and the otherworldly, infusing each stroke with a sense of wonder and possibility. Whether it's crafting sleek starships, sprawling alien landscapes, or enigmatic creatures from the far reaches of the cosmos, I relish the opportunity to explore new frontiers and push the boundaries of visual storytelling.


Level Designer

Level design is where art and gameplay intersect, and as a level designer, I revel in the intricate dance between form and function. From meticulously sculpting terrain to crafting intricate puzzles and encounters, I strive to create environments that not only look stunning but also provide players with a rich and rewarding gameplay experience. Whether it's guiding them through a sprawling metropolis or plunging them into the depths of a long-forgotten ruin, I'm dedicated to crafting levels that captivate the imagination and keep players coming back for more.


Sci-Fi is My Passion

As an avid fan of science fiction, I'm endlessly fascinated by the boundless possibilities of the genre. From dystopian futures to sprawling space operas, I draw inspiration from the works of visionaries past and present, weaving together elements of technology, exploration, and human drama to create worlds that feel both familiar and utterly alien. Whether I'm exploring the furthest reaches of the cosmos or delving into the mysteries of the human psyche, I'm always eager to embark on new adventures and push the boundaries of what's possible in interactive storytelling.
