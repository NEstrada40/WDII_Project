About Me

Welcome to my corner! I'm Nathan, a fledgling game developer with a passion for crafting immersive experiences and stories. With a deep interest in level design, worldbuilding and stories, I hope to be able to give back to the gaming community that has made me who I am, one game at a time.


Level Designer

I believe that balancing in a game starts with the ground you walk on. The feeling of oppression or freedom that a level's shape gives is key to the player experience, and in the coming years I hope to deepen my relationship with the world of level design. Level design is the foundation of mechanics, but it's often also where the story starts. Every step of the way, to ensure my games shine, my goal will be to deliver all that I can of a designer's vision.


Worldbuilder

With a primary focus on fiction, creative writing is one of my greatest personal pleasures when I'm away from Unity or Unreal. As a story builder, my ideal is to breathe life into game worlds by using the minute details on the walls and character dialogue. I relish the opportunity to explore new frontiers and push the boundaries of what constitutes storytelling.


My Interests, My Passions

As an avid consumer of modern fantasy and scifi, I find the boundless possibilities of fiction fascinating and ample resources to work with. Games are where I was raised, and with nearly two decades of play under my thumb, the future I envision is one by gamers, for games. I'm always eager to embark on new adventures and push the boundaries of what's possible in interactive storytelling. I'm always ready for more of what's great.
