I worked on IMPETUS as a solo developer and level designer.

Contributions

  • Conceptualized IMPETUS, a fast-paced inertia-based platforming game based in Unity
  • Established the pixel art 2D side-scrolling platformer identity for IMPETUS

What is IMPETUS?

  • The ultimate vision of IMPETUS is equal parts story and gameplay, with a definitive momentum mechanic that capitalizes on fast-paced gameplay to increase player lethality... and mortality
  • A story aiming to intrigue with complex themes and characters
  • Roguelite, Platformer. What will make IMPETUS different is its focus on character-warped gameplay, providing replayability and retention

Stories Built

  • Great stories can begin and end with their villains — An underground organization is bent on changing the world, one technological advancement at a time
  • Uneasy alliances and various philosophical and political gains means the world is less fated to be saved than one would think
