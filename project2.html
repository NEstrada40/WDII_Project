I worked for PLACEHOLDER as Game/Concept Artist for IMPETUS.

Conceptualization and Vision

  • Contributed to the conceptualization of IMPETUS's vast and procedurally generated universe, envisioning diverse planetary landscapes, flora, and fauna.
  • Worked closely with the art director and design team to establish the visual identity and aesthetic principles that would guide the game's art direction.

Concept Art

  • Produced concept art for various planetary biomes, ranging from lush forests and sprawling deserts to icy tundras and volcanic regions.
  • Designed alien creatures and plant life, incorporating elements of realism and imagination to create unique and otherworldly species.
  • Created concept sketches and mood boards to explore different art styles and visual themes, informing the development of in-game assets and environments.

Asset Creation

  • Modeled and textured environmental assets, including terrain features, rock formations, vegetation, and architectural structures, using 3D software.
  • Collaborated with the technical art team to optimize assets for performance and compatibility across different platforms and hardware configurations.
  • Created texture maps and material shaders to achieve realistic surface properties and visual effects, enhancing the immersion of players in the game world.

World Building

  • Assisted in the development of procedural generation algorithms and tools for creating planetary landscapes and ecosystems, ensuring variety and coherence across procedurally generated content.
  • Participated in the design and layout of in-game environments, including terrain generation, placement of assets, and creation of points of interest and landmarks.
  • Iterated on world-building assets and parameters based on feedback from playtesting and user experience testing, refining the visual and gameplay aspects of IMPETUS's universe.
