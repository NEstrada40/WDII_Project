I worked on Station Salvation II as a solo developer and level designer.

Contributions

  • Developed a sequel to a previous team project made in Unreal Engine 4, constructing previous mechanics from scratch
  • Designed a level, outlining the player's role in combating threats and protecting the system and themself

What is Station Salvation II?

  • A third-person survival sci-fi horror shooter taking place on a space station adrift in space
  • To survive, it will take the player's determination and skill in melee and ranged combat to escape the consequences of an alien invader

Level Design

  • Both in aesthetic and atmosphere, the futuristic technological environment takes advantage of the stark contrast between perpetually activated lighting and smooth architecture and a total lack of friendly life to create the element of oppressive loneliness that is present throughout the game
  • Claustrophobic environments allow for constant ambushes that will put the player on edge and further aid in the feeling of suspense and instinct to protect oneself necessary to enhance the experience
  • Designed levels with branching pathways to encourage exploration and replayability
  • Dynamic level elements and triggered map actions introduce an additional element of strategy to the player, allowing for player resourcefulness to turn the tables in a game about constantly being caught in traps
